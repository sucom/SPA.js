Extensibility

SPAjs is built on eXtreme Programming Principles (KISS and YAGNI). Hence, the core team has decided to keep the framework simple and light. However, on need basis it can be extended with suitable libraries.

This section gives some pointers to such libraries.

NOTE: The libraries mentioned in this section are FYI only. It's developers choice to choose the recommended or choose any other alternates. SPAjs does not support the recommended libraries directly.
